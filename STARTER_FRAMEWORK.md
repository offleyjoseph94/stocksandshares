# £1,000 Starter Framework

> **Not financial advice.** This is a personal research/tracking framework. Every name here can fall; the aggressive and moonshot buckets can lose most or all of their value. You decide what to actually buy. Re-verify every ticker, price and broker availability live before buying.

**Profile:** UK retail · primary broker Revolut UK, fallback Trading 212 · starting amount **£1,000** · deploy **phased over ~3 months** (~£333/month) to average out the entry price.

Both Revolut and Trading 212 support **fractional shares** and commission-free buys, so small per-name amounts work fine. Pick one buy day each month and stick to it — removes emotion.

---

## The four buckets

| Bucket | Role | Hold horizon | Mindset |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Steady** | Diversified core (ETF) | 3–5+ yrs | Boring is good. The ballast. |
| **Balanced** | Proven, still growing | 1–3 yrs | Conviction with a floor. |
| **Aggressive** | High-growth, unproven | 1–3 yrs | Expect 50% drawdowns. |
| **Moonshot** | Lottery ticket | 3+ yrs or write-off | Money you can lose entirely. |

## How to run it
- **Hold to horizon.** Reassess a name only if its *thesis* breaks (see the dashboard's risk notes), not on a normal dip.
- **Watch the Monday refresh.** The dashboard's catalyst/risk notes auto-update every Monday morning.
- **Don't:** check daily · chase a name after a big spike · "average down" on a moonshot because it's cheap · let any single moonshot grow past its bucket without trimming.
- **Top-ups later:** when you add new money, feed it into whichever bucket is below its target % — this rebalances automatically.
